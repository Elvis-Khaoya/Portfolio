About Me

I’m Elvis Wanyonyi Khaoya, a dedicated IT Support Technician and Cybersecurity Enthusiast based in Nairobi, Kenya. I specialize in troubleshooting hardware, software, and network issues, ensuring systems run smoothly and securely across multiple environments.

With hands-on experience from roles at Avenue Healthcare and the County Government of Nyeri, I’ve built strong expertise in system administration, IT infrastructure support, and network security. I’m passionate about helping organizations maintain reliable systems while strengthening their cybersecurity posture through proactive monitoring, user training, and vulnerability assessments.

My technical background spans across Windows, Linux, and macOS environments, with proficiency in tools such as Nmap, Wireshark, Burp Suite, and Metasploit. I’m also skilled in Python, Java, and PHP, with a strong foundation in cloud computing, virtualization, and Active Directory management.

I hold a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from St. Paul’s University, graduating with Second Class Honors (Upper Division), and a Diploma in Computer Science from the same institution. I’ve also completed certifications from Cisco and IBM covering Networking, Cybersecurity, and Cloud Fundamentals.

I thrive in fast-paced environments that challenge me to think critically, solve problems creatively, and continuously learn new technologies. My mission is to combine my technical expertise and cybersecurity awareness to make digital spaces safer and more reliable.
